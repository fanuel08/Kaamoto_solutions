üçö Rice (No-Burn Method)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice (e.g., Basmati or Pishori)
  • 4 cups water (or 1:2 ratio)
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Procedure

  1. Boil water, add washed rice, salt and oil as desired.
  2. Bring to a boil on high heat. Stir once to prevent sticking.
  3. Simmer for 7 minutes till water is absorbed and pores form.
  4. Immediately transfer to KaaMoto Bag, tightly covered.
  5. Allow the rice to steam for 25‚Äì30 minutes.
  6. Open the bag, fluff with a fork, and serve.
