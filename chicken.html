🐓 Kienyeji Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 whole kienyeji chicken, cleaned and cut into pieces
  • Water to cover
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: onions, garlic, ginger for flavor

Procedure

  1. Place chicken pieces in a sufuria and cover with water.
  2. Add salt and aromatics if desired.
  3. Bring to a rolling boil for 40–50 minutes.
  4. Transfer to KaaMoto Bag, tightly sealed.
  5. Allow to cook passively for 3–4 hours until the meat is tender.
  6. Remove and fry or stew as preferred.

Initial Boil Time: 40–50 minutes

KaaMoto Cook Time: 3–4 hours
