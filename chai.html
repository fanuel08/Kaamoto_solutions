☕ Kenyan Tea (Chai)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups water
  • 2–3 tsp tea leaves
  • Sugar to taste
  • Optional: ginger, cardamom, or masala spices

Procedure

  1. Combine milk, water, tea leaves, and sugar in a sufuria.
  2. Add spices if desired and bring to a rolling boil for 5–7 minutes.
  3. Strain if needed, then cover tightly and place in the KaaMoto Bag.
  4. Tea will remain piping hot for 1–2 hours, ready to serve anytime.

Initial Boil Time: 5–7 minutes

KaaMoto Keep-Warm Time: 1–2 hours
